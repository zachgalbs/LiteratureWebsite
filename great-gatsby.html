The Great Gatsby

About the Book

The Great Gatsby is a novel by F. Scott Fitzgerald, published in 1925. The book is set in the Jazz Age and tells the story of Jay Gatsby's unrequited love for Daisy Buchanan. The novel explores themes of the American Dream, wealth, and social stratification.
